Lury

A 2D platform game developed in 3 months using the Construct Engine. You can play it here.

  • Worked in a team as storyliner, game designer, level designer and programmer. Assisted team members from other areas (art and sound) with ideas and solutions for the (innumerable) problems that showed up.
  • Created the game's storyline using Graciliano Ramos' book "Barren Lives" as an inspiration.
  • Programmed the behaviour of enemies (bats, spiders, scorpions) and their interaction with the player (sensing that player is close and then attacking).
  • Programmed the behavior of the rock (rolling on the surface and destroying objects - the camera follows the movement of the rock).
  • Programmed player's interaction with the mushrooms (player jumps on them and they behave like a trampoline).
  • Designed and programmed an in-game puzzle (player has to find a way to open a cage and release an owl).

Kitty vs Doggos

I made this game on Scratch as an assigment for the CS50's Introduction to Computer Science course.

  • When I designed the game I had a few things in mind: it should have multiple characters and incorporate a simple, yet cohesive narrative structure.
  • To address the storytelling challenge, I drew inspiration from classic TV cartoons that show animal dynamics (like Tom & Jerry). This familiar framework provided an intuitive foundation that players could immediately understand and connect with.
  • Programmed the behaviour for each one of the three characters, focusing on movement mechanics, interaction protocols, and response systems.
